# Autotelic Personality

My Autotelic personality keeps me constantly occupied exploring, learning new skills, and challenging myself for the sake of the challenge. While DJ-ing, the challenge is to set and manipulate the vibes while maintaining technical composure. In Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, it's the persistence of trial and error, constantly adapting leverage strategies to overcome opponents with different styles and body types. With making Sourdough, the challenge is understanding the signals for how much moisture to add to the dough as well as how long to let it rise. Everyday presents a challenge, and every challenge presents an opportunity to rise up to the occasion and possibly to learn.
