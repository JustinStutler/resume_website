## How This Portfolio Works

This site implements [Andrej Karpathy's LLM Wiki](https://gist.github.com/karpathy/442a6bf555914893e9891c11519de94f) concept. Instead of traditional RAG that retrieves raw document chunks, the system maintains a curated, interlinked knowledge base that synthesizes information across sources.

As Karpathy puts it: *"The tedious part of maintaining a knowledge base is not the reading or thinking, it's the bookkeeping."* The LLM handles the bookkeeping. The human curates the sources.

---

### Three Layers of Knowledge

| Layer | What It Contains |
|---|---|
| Raw Sources | Immutable originals like Justin's resume, statement of purpose, transcripts, GRE scores, and personal background |
| The Wiki | Synthesized pages that cross reference information across sources, giving each project, skill, and experience its own structured page |
| The Schema | Conventions that define how pages are organized, tagged, and indexed using YAML frontmatter in an Obsidian compatible vault |

---

### The Three Stage Pipeline

When you ask a question, three things happen on the backend.

| Stage | What Happens |
|---|---|
| Scope Guard | A fast Gemini call determines if the question is about Justin. Off topic queries get a polite redirect so the AI stays focused |
| Context Selection | Gemini reads the vault index (titles and tags of every wiki page) and selects the pages relevant to your question. This uses semantic understanding, not keyword matching |
| Answer Generation | The selected pages are bundled into a prompt. Gemini generates an answer grounded exclusively in that context, ensuring accuracy |

---

### Why This Beats Traditional RAG

| Traditional RAG | LLM Wiki (This Site) |
|---|---|
| Chunks lose document context | Synthesized pages preserve full context |
| No cross referencing | Tags and links connect related topics |
| Retrieval depends on embedding similarity | LLM powered semantic page selection |
| Static chunks | Wiki compounds and improves over time |

---

### Tech Stack

| Component | Technology |
|---|---|
| AI | Google Gemini (gemini 2.5 flash lite) |
| Knowledge Base | Obsidian compatible markdown vault |
| Backend | Python Flask on Render |
| Frontend | Vanilla JS with ES6 modules on Netlify |

This is a fully deployed production application, not a demo or sandbox.
